Frequently Asked
How do I become an EviEarner?
Upgrade from Membership. EviEarner unlocks the Marketplace and Cashout, letting you withdraw your UserPoints (1,000 pts = $0.10).
How does Cashout work?
Cashout converts UserPoints to Dollars. A 10% platform fee applies, so you keep 90%. Payouts are sent monthly via EviEarner Payments to your linked bank account.
What fee applies to eGifts?
Sending an eGift carries a 20% fee. The recipient receives the remaining value as UserPoints in their wallet.
How do I get a verified badge?
Apply via Get Verified in the menu. Submit a valid government ID. Review usually takes 2–3 business days.
How do I secure my account?
Enable Two-Factor Authentication and login alerts in Security & Login, and review your active sessions regularly.

Your privacy matters. This explains what we collect, why, and the control you have. Plain language, no surprises.

What we collect

Account details (name, email, phone), content you create, marketplace and wallet activity, and basic device/usage data to keep the app secure and working.

How we use it

To run your account, power the feed and Discover, process Marketplace orders and Cashout, prevent fraud, and improve EVINIE. We don't sell your personal data.

What we share

Only what's needed: payment processors for funding/cashout, and other users when you post, message, or transact. We share with authorities only when legally required.

Your choices

Control who sees your posts with the privacy selector, manage alerts in Notifications, block accounts, and edit or download your info. You can delete your account anytime.

Security

We protect your data with encryption and offer Two-Factor Authentication. No system is perfect — use a strong password and review your active sessions.

Data retention

We keep your data while your account is active. After deletion, we remove or anonymise it, except where we must keep records (e.g. transactions) by law.

Children

EVINIE is not for anyone under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We'll post updates here and notify you of significant changes.
